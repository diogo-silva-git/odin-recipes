Bitoque

The Prego, also known as bitoque do prego, called prego no prato when served as a dish or prego no pao when served as a sandwich.

(Portuguese: Bitoque de Prego) is a typical Portuguese dish.

It consists of a small beef patty which is normally served as a standalone dish or a sandwich, sometimes on a bolo do caco.

Ingredients

  • 1 steak of your choosing (nothing overly thick)
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup white or green wine
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon pepper paste
  • 1 bay Leaf
  • Salt/Pepper to taste
  • French Fries
  • White rice
  • Salad
